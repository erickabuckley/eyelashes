What we offer:

Our eyelash extensions are semi-permanent fibres that are glued to your natural lashes to make them appear longer, thicker and darker.

Our Lash extensions are totally customizable depending on the wearer's eye shapes and tastes and they can look from very natural to uber dramatic.

Our trained technichian are total pros in applying lash extensions to a person's natural eyelashes. The extensions enhance what's already there, and the lashes don't require mascara. Professional lash extensions differ from the DIY temporary eyelashes people can buy in the drugstore.
